SeatSure

Site Purpose

The website will serve as a user-friendly platform for booking tickets to concerts and live events. It will feature event listings, ticket availability, pricing details, and a secure checkout process. Users will be able to filter events by date, location, or category, and receive e-tickets upon purchase. Additionally, the site may include event descriptions, artist details, and customer reviews.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: "How can I find and book tickets for my favorite events?"

Scenario 1: "How can I find and book tickets for my favorite events?" A visitor, Lisa, is looking for an easy way to book tickets for an upcoming concert. She lands on the website and sees a search bar on the homepage where she types the artist’s name. The website displays a list of upcoming concerts with details like date, venue, and ticket availability. Lisa clicks on the event, views the interactive seating chart, selects her preferred seat, and proceeds to checkout. After payment, she receives a confirmation email with her e-ticket.

Website Features That Address This Scenario:

  • A search function to find events quickly.
  • A clear and organized event listing page.
  • An interactive seat selection system.
  • A secure payment gateway for smooth transactions.
  • Instant email confirmation and e-ticket delivery.
Scenario 2: "What if I need to cancel or modify my ticket?"

Mark booked tickets for a theater show but later realized he can't attend. He visits the website and logs into his user dashboard. There, he finds a section labeled "My Tickets", where he can view all his bookings. The site allows him to either cancel his ticket for a refund (based on the event's cancellation policy) or transfer it to another person. He chooses to transfer the ticket to a friend, who receives an email confirmation with the updated details.

Website Features That Address This Scenario:

  • A user dashboard to manage bookings.
  • An easy ticket cancellation and refund system.
  • An easy ticket cancellation and refund system.
  • A ticket transfer option for flexibility
  • Clear cancellation policies displayed before purchase.
